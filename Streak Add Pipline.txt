Q1. Creating a pipeline in streak

Go to streak.com.
Click on Add to chrome button, add extension in chrome .
Now to Gmail account.
You get Streak icon on right corner to sign in with streak to active in this Gmail account.
Sign in with Google account in stream from gmail dashboard.
Then you get popup choose CRM in that and rest are anything..
You will get Streak pipline in left side panel.

Click on streak pipline + button to create custom pipeline
	Select custom
	Add Stages and column field name
	Click on create.
	
	Then click on + sign on right corner to Create box, add a box.
You can send boxes to another stage by change stage from stage dropdown column.
